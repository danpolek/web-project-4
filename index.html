About Naawi-Oodena

Our Vision

Creating a vibrant, sustainable community that honors Indigenous traditions while providing modern urban living.

Location

156-acre urban reserve development on the former Kapyong Barracks site in Winnipeg, Manitoba.

Development

$1 billion project representing the largest single return of urban land to First Nations in Canadian history.
