About Naawi-Oodena

Naawi-Oodena is a unique urban reserve located on the former Kapyong Barracks site in Winnipeg, Manitoba. Led by Treaty One Nations, it symbolizes peace, unity, and reconciliation. "Naawi-Oodena" means "Centre of the Heart" in Anishinaabemowin, and it reflects the vision of Indigenous-led development.

Source: Treaty1.ca
