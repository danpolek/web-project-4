Welcome to Naawi-Oodena

Naawi-Oodena is an extraordinary urban reserve located on the former Kapyong Barracks site in Winnipeg, Manitoba. Led by Treaty One Nations, it symbolizes peace, unity, and reconciliation. "Naawi-Oodena" means "Centre of the Heart" in Anishinaabemowin, and it reflects the vision of Indigenous-led development.

Learn more from Treaty One

Main Development Areas

  • Residential Living: A welcoming neighborhood for Indigenous and non-Indigenous residents alike.
  • Mixed-use Commercial Space: Shops, restaurants, and services that create economic opportunities.
  • Community and Culture: Gathering spaces to celebrate Indigenous identity, stories, and traditions.
  • Recreation and Sports: Green spaces, walking paths, and places for sports and wellness.
  • Education and Learning: Schools and cultural learning centers for all ages.

Why Naawi-Oodena Matters

This site sets a new standard for Indigenous urban development. It empowers local communities, honors history, and strengthens the connection between Indigenous culture and the land.
