Residential Living at Naawi-Oodena

A welcoming community blending urban convenience with Indigenous culture

Your Home in the Heart of the Community

Naawi-Oodena offers diverse housing options designed to meet the needs of all community members. Our residential areas combine modern urban living with the rich cultural heritage of Treaty One Nations.

The development features a mix of housing types, from single-family homes to modern apartments, all designed with sustainability and community connection in mind.
