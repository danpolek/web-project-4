Residential Living at Naawi-Oodena

Urban convenience meets Indigenous culture and sustainable design

Your Home in the Community

Naawi-Oodena offers diverse housing options designed with Indigenous values of community and connection to land. Our residential areas blend modern living with cultural heritage, creating functional yet meaningful spaces.

The development features a mix of housing types, all designed sustainably. Neighborhoods use circular layouts promoting social interaction and nature connection, with communal green spaces for recreation and traditional uses.

Cultural Design Elements

Our designs incorporate Indigenous knowledge and values.

Sacred Geometry

Circular neighborhood layouts reflect traditional camp designs, with east-facing entrances welcoming the morning sun.

Natural Materials

We use wood, stone and other traditional materials sourced sustainably, blending ancient and contemporary elements.

Storytelling Art

Public art shares Treaty One Nations' history and teachings, with interactive installations throughout.
